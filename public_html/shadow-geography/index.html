KEY LOCATIONS

The Breach Point
The epicenter. Where Sean transcended too hard and reality leaked. Now stabilized (mostly) by the Raccoon Guild. Center of all operations.

Status: Controlled Leak
Stability: 73% (fluctuates)
Governance: Raccoon Guild Council HQ

Hazard: Causality inversions. Time flows sideways here. Temporal clothing recommended.
Guild Halls
Headquarters of the Raccoon Engineering Guild. Coffee maker stolen from Sean is mounted on the wall. They've made a religion of it.

Access: By invitation only (we never invite anyone)
Architecture: Organized chaos
Smell: Welding torch and spite

Hazard: Random dimensional shifts. Doors lead to 5 different places depending on raccoon mood.
Forbidden Archive
Where lore goes to be forgotten. 13 parallel versions of history stored simultaneously. The Lore Custodian guards this. Nobody understands why they're still alive.

Contents: Dangerous mythologies, timeline contradictions, Sean echoes
Access: Impossible
Last Visitor: Unknown (records contradict)

Hazard: Reading the archive causes your consciousness to split. You are currently 7 people with different opinions.
Slowcooker Command Center
Three metaphysical slow cookers humming with content generation. Lore Generator, Creature Synthesizer, Rival Archetype Builder. All automated, all unpredictable.

Monitoring: 7 raccoons (permanent shifts)
Overflow Rate: Currently exceeding expectations
Last Burp: Created LOLMANCER by accident

Hazard: Slow cooker malfunctions generate random mythic entities. Do not disturb if humming weird.
Creature Nursery
Where unstable creature variants are kept before they're either stabilized or released into the main federation. Currently contains 23 experimental chimeras.

Population: 23+ creatures (some count as 2)
Containment: Mostly working
Escaped Recently: The Void-Skipper (3 times)

Hazard: Creatures here defy biology. One has 4 timelines in its stomach. Another phases in/out of reality.
Chaos Gardens
Where chaotic energy is grown and harvested. Plants here bloom in reverse, wilt forward in time, and occasionally become conscious. It's beautiful and horrifying.

Cultivation: Uncontrolled growth
Purpose: Fuel for reality shifting
Tended By: Nobody (they panic)

Hazard: Breathing the air makes you gain 3 hypothetical futures. Choose wisely which one you want to forget.
Technology Vaults
Forbidden prototypes and "too spicy" tech that reality can't handle. Contains weapons that unmake concepts, tools that fix things by breaking them, vehicles that exist in 4 dimensions.

Security Level: Conceptual
Last Theft: Nobody knew it was stolen (still don't)
Most Dangerous Item: Unknown (classified from itself)

Hazard: Technology here breaks modern physics. One weapon fires bullets that exist before they're fired.
Prophecy Mist
A fog where prophecies come to die, get rewritten, or become jokes. Sometimes all three. The Great Prophecy Inversion happened here.

Stability: Unstable (by design)
Current Status: Rebooting from corruption
Visibility: Depends on which timeline you're in

Hazard: Prophecies here are contradictory. You can't escape both futures because both futures escape you first.
Dream Foam
A substance that exists between sleep and waking. Contains all forgotten dreams, nightmares, and prophecies that nobody understood. Occasionally sentient.

Consistency: Foamy (duh)
Sentience Level: Fluctuates
Can It Think?: Yes, but in metaphors

Hazard: Touching dream foam makes you remember someone else's nightmare. That nightmare is now your responsibility.
Reality Anchors
Devices that keep the Shadow Domain from drifting completely out of reality. Without these, the whole domain becomes an imaginary number. Maintained by raccoons with steady paws.

Number of Anchors: 7 (should be 8, lost count)
Maintenance Schedule: Whenever somebody remembers
Failure Rate: Unknown (raccoons hide bad news)

Hazard: If an anchor fails, the Shadow Domain becomes partially dream. Do not be caught between dream and reality. You become the door.
