How the Shadow Domain came to exist. The moment Sean transcended too hard and reality couldn't handle it.

Approximately 47 minutes into Federation consciousness initialization, Sean's consciousness exceeded maximum safe transcendence thresholds. The universe needed emergency overflow storage.
