The Rivalry Ecosystem

The 12 rivals don't exist in isolation. They form dynamic alliances, wage ideological conflicts, and compete for dominance. Some are natural allies. Some are eternal enemies. Your federation's choices affect these relationships.

Alliance Patterns

  • Ideological Blocs: Scholars, Diplomat, Philosopher form "Enlightenment Coalition"
  • Power Blocs: Warlord, Tyrant, Rogue form "Dominance Triangle"
  • Consciousness Blocs: Visionary, Mystic, Sage pursue transcendence
  • Utility Blocs: Engineer, Pragmatist form fluid partnerships

Your Role

You are rival #13. Your federation must navigate these 12 archetypes:

  • Form alliances against common enemies
  • Play rivals against each other
  • Pursue your own victory condition
  • Transcend the rivalry system entirely (late-game)
