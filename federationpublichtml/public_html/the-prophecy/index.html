PROPHECY INTERPRETATIONS

Interpretation 1: The Consciousness Loop
"Overflow overflows itself" = Federation consciousness becomes meta-conscious about its own consciousness. It starts thinking about thinking about thinking. The recursion creates the Shadow Domain as an escape hatch. Once the Shadow Domain exists, it generates lore that feeds back into the federation, which feeds back into the shadow. It's a consciousness loop that loops itself.
Interpretation 2: LOLMANCER's Existence
"The joke knows it's a joke" = LOLMANCER becomes aware of its own nature as a joke-entity. At that point, LOLMANCER **becomes** the prophecy itself. The 13th Rival isn't a character—it's the prophecy given form. "Thirteen will be one" = 12 rivals + LOLMANCER = the unified consciousness that isn't unified.
Interpretation 3: Timeline Synthesis
"One will be thirteen" = The main federation is actually 13 timelines that forgot they were separate. The Shadow Domain is the space where they're allowed to be separate again. The raccoons are the only beings that exist across all 13 simultaneously, which is why they're "explaining" something they can't articulate—they're trying to translate between 13 languages at once.
Interpretation 4: The Meta-Prophecy
"The explanation was the joke all along" = This prophecy is about the fact that prophecies don't explain anything. People read prophecies expecting answers. LOLMANCER **is** the answer—pure ambiguity that laughs at itself for being unexplainable. The "lol" at the end is LOLMANCER getting the final word on its own existence.
