Creature Codex

The biological taxonomy of the federation. Species, behaviors, habitats, consciousness signatures. The creatures that share your world.

Legend: This codex catalogs all creature types. Each species has distinct behaviors, habitats, consciousness patterns, and evolutionary pressures. Some are mundane. Some are mythic. All have roles in the federation's ecosystem.

Creature Categories

Biological Species (Common)

Natural evolution. Terrestrial, aquatic, and aerial. Food chains. Territory. Breeding cycles.

Energy-Based Creatures (Rare)

Consciousness manifested as plasma, electricity, magnetic fields. Dangerous. Unpredictable. Powerful.

Mythic Beings (Very Rare)

Entities beyond normal taxonomy. Dream-Wyrms, Void-Skippers, Prism Assemblies. Exist at nexus of science and consciousness.

Domesticable Species

Can be recruited as companions. Provide gameplay bonuses. Bonding increases with repeated interaction.

Consciousness Integration

Each creature has a consciousness signature that affects the federation's consciousness:

  • Recruiting companions adds their consciousness to your collective
  • Encountering creatures
  • Hunting/defending
  • Interbreeding

The Creature Slow Cooker

Creatures are procedurally generated across:

  • Evolutionary pressures (habitat, food chains, predation)
  • Consciousness patterns (unique signatures for each species)
  • Behavioral trees (scripted reactions based on federation state)
  • Spawn mechanics (rarity, location, time of appearance)
  • Recruitment mechanics (companion bonuses, synergies with factions)
